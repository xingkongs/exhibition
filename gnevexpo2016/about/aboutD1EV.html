关于第一电动

第一电动网创立于2010年6月，隶属于北京智电未来信息科技有限公司，以成长中的中国新能源汽车市场为依托，在全球范围内整合产业链资源，通过网络、会展、杂志等多媒体形态，推动全球新能源汽车市场的商业及信息交流。

我们面向全球主要新能源汽车供应商、经销商和消费者，提供强大的产品及交易信息数据库服务，以及涉及政策、技术、市 场三大方向的政策解读服务。

在此基础上，我们的专业化团队将根据用户的需求，提供品牌、营销、投融资等定制化咨询服务。最后，我们将面向消费者 提供产品评测及交易服务。

我们的定位是：专注建设中国最权威、最专业的新能源汽车产业信息与数据服务平台

我们的宗旨是：为推动新能源汽车的普及而竭尽所能

我们的价值观是：客户第一、追求专业、拥抱变化、合作创新

D1ev.com, a B2B website, was established in June 2010, following the development of China new energy vehicle market. We collect and integrate the world-wide new energy vehicle information to push the global new energy vehicle market and industrial techcom- munication, through network, exhibitions, magazines and other multimedia.

With the needs of main global new energy vehicle suppliers, dealers and consumers, we have been building a strong trade infor- mation warehouse, which includes related analyses on new energy policies, markets and technologies.

Besides, our professional group provides custom service as consumers required, like consulting service of sales and investment, what is more, we provide product test report and trading service for them.

What we do: We are focusing on being the most professional and authoritative new energy vehicle information service platform.

Our Objective: Do our best for the worldwide popularization of new energy vehicle.

Enterprise Core Values: Customer first, Profession, Teamwork, Innovation.
